Dubai Police Thwarts Major Phone Scam Operation

March 20, 2024

By Dubai Police

Dubai Police Dismantles International Phone Scam Network

In a major breakthrough for public safety, Dubai Police successfully dismantled a sophisticated phone scam network, thanks to crucial information provided by a vigilant South African national. The operation led to multiple arrests and prevented potential victims from falling prey to fraudulent schemes.

"The cooperation between international communities and law enforcement is crucial in combating cyber fraud. This case exemplifies how public vigilance can help protect countless potential victims."

Dubai Police Spokesperson

The Operation

The investigation began when a South African national reported suspicious activities involving fake prize winning schemes. Dubai Police's cybercrime unit quickly mobilized, utilizing advanced tracking techniques to locate and apprehend the perpetrators.

The Scam Methodology

The criminals operated by:

  • Contacting victims with false promises of lottery winnings
  • Requesting advance fees for prize processing
  • Using sophisticated methods to appear legitimate
  • Targeting victims across multiple countries

Public Safety Advisory

Dubai Police urges the public to remain vigilant and remember:

  • Legitimate organizations never request payment to claim prizes
  • Be suspicious of unexpected prize notifications
  • Never share personal or financial information with unknown callers
  • Report suspicious activities immediately

Cybercrime Prevention

Dubai Police continues to enhance its cybercrime prevention capabilities and public awareness programs to protect residents from various forms of online fraud.
