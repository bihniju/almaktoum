Meet Our Project Coordinator

"My name is Daniela Victoria, and thanks to the help and collaboration of His Royal Highness, I may in the future carry out a beautiful dream, turned into a project to provide a grain of sand for future generations to come."

About the Project

This ambitious project aims to reopen and transform an abandoned facility in Argentina into a Zonal Acute School Hospital, equipped with all specialties and cutting-edge technology. The facility, formerly known as the "Israeli Home," has a rich history of providing shelter and support to Holocaust survivors and their children.

The Vision

With 13 years of experience as a nurse at the hospital where she was born, Daniela Victoria is passionate about giving back to the community. The project seeks to revitalize this historic space, currently filled with abandoned cars and environmental concerns, into a state-of-the-art medical facility that will serve the local community for generations to come.

"It is my way of giving back a little of the help that those angels gave me, leaving my mark of solidarity for the next generations."

Daniela Victoria
