Our Story

The Donald J. Trump Foundation , Non-Profit Organization

Established in 1987 in NY - Founded by Donald J. Trump, who is the 45th President of the United States of America. Mr. Trump is also a real estate developer, television personality, author, and the Chairman and C.E.O. of the Trump Organization, a U.S.-based real-estate development company. Some of Trump's properties include Trump Tower, AXA Financial Center, and Trump International Hotel and Tower. Trump is also the founder of Trump Entertainment Resorts, which operates numerous casinos and hotels across the world. In addition, Mr. Trump was also the host and Executive Producer of the hit television reality show, "The Apprentice." Trump has authored numerous books including "The America We Deserve" (2000), "Trump 101: The Way to Success" (2007), and "Trump Never Give Up: How I Turned My Biggest Challenges into Success" (2008
